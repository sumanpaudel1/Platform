FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

How do I get started with Clothy?

Simply sign up for a free account, verify your email, and follow our guided setup process. You can have your own online store up and running in as little as 15 minutes.

How does the subscription billing work?

You can choose between monthly, quarterly (3-month), or annual (12-month) billing cycles. Longer subscriptions offer savings compared to monthly payments. All payments are securely processed via eSewa.

How does the eSewa integration work?

Our platform seamlessly connects with eSewa for both your subscription payments and your customer transactions. All transaction details are available in your dashboard for easy reconciliation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can change your subscription plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll gain immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
