FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our vendor platform

How do I get started with Clothy?

Simply sign up for a free account, verify your email, and follow our guided setup process. You can have your own online store up and running in as little as 15 minutes.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes! While free plans come with a subdomain (yourstore.platform.com), Professional and Enterprise plans support custom domains that you own (yourstore.com).

How does the AI product recommendation work?

Our AI engine analyzes product images, descriptions, customer browsing patterns, and purchase history to suggest relevant products to your customers, increasing engagement and sales.

What payment methods can my customers use?

We support various payment methods including credit/debit cards, eSewa, and cash on delivery. All transactions are securely processed and monitored for fraud protection.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can change your subscription plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll gain immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
